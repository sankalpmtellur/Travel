Discover the Wonders of Karnataka

Karnataka, located in southern India, is a land of diverse landscapes, rich history, and vibrant culture. From ancient temples and stunning palaces to serene beaches and lush forests, Karnataka offers a perfect blend of tradition and modernity.

Top Destinations

Bengaluru

The Silicon Valley of India, known for its parks, nightlife, and booming IT industry.

Mysore

Famous for its royal heritage, palaces, and the vibrant Dasara festival.

Hampi

A UNESCO World Heritage Site with stunning ruins of the Vijayanagara Empire.

Coorg

Known as the Scotland of India, offering lush coffee plantations and misty hills.

Udupi

A coastal town renowned for its temples, beaches, and authentic South Indian cuisine.

Badami

Known for its rock-cut temples and ancient architecture.

Shivanasamudra Falls

A breathtaking waterfall surrounded by lush greenery.

Bijapur

Home to historic monuments and the iconic Gol Gumbaz.
