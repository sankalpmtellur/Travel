Discover the Wonders of Meghalaya

Meghalaya, known as the "Abode of Clouds," is a northeastern state of India famous for its breathtaking landscapes, unique culture, and lush greenery. It is a paradise for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Top Destinations

Cherrapunji

Known as one of the wettest places on earth, Cherrapunji is famous for its living root bridges and stunning waterfalls.

Shillong

The capital city of Meghalaya, also called the "Scotland of the East," offers charming landscapes and a vibrant culture.

Mawsynram

The wettest place on Earth, offering mesmerizing views and lush greenery.

Dawki

A quaint town known for its crystal-clear river and the India-Bangladesh border.

Nohkalikai Falls

One of India's tallest waterfalls, offering breathtaking views of the cascading water and surrounding cliffs.

Mawlynnong

Dubbed the cleanest village in Asia, it showcases sustainable living and beautiful surroundings.

Umiam Lake

A beautiful man-made reservoir surrounded by lush green hills, ideal for boating and picnics.

Khasi Hills

Famous for its scenic beauty, traditional villages, and the unique matrilineal culture of the Khasi tribe.
