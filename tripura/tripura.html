Discover the Wonders of Tripura

Tripura, a state rich in history, culture, and nature, offers a variety of scenic landscapes, ancient temples, waterfalls, and peaceful hills that make it a perfect travel destination for nature lovers and explorers.

Top Destinations

Neermahal Palace

A stunning water palace located on the Rudrasagar Lake, blending Mughal and Hindu architectural styles.

Unakoti

Ancient rock-cut sculptures and carvings, depicting gods and goddesses, set amidst the tranquil hills.

Kamala Sagar Lake

A picturesque lake near the Kamala Sagar temple, offering a serene view with historical significance.

Jampui Hills

Known as the "Land of Eternal Spring," this hill station offers pleasant weather, tea gardens, and breathtaking views.

Twungwra Waterfall

A stunning waterfall nestled in the forests, providing an exotic and peaceful escape into nature.

Rijang Waterfall

A hidden gem of Tripura, Rijang waterfall offers a serene experience amidst lush greenery and clear waters.

Trishna Wildlife Sanctuary

Known for its rich biodiversity, this sanctuary is home to various species of flora and fauna, making it a perfect spot for nature enthusiasts.

Buddha Stupa

Located in the center of the state, this stupa is a peaceful Buddhist pilgrimage site surrounded by lush greenery.
