Discover the Wonders of Mizoram

Mizoram, nestled in the northeastern part of India, is a land of rolling hills, pristine landscapes, and vibrant culture. Known for its hospitable people, Mizoram offers a unique blend of natural beauty and traditional charm.

Top Destinations

Aizawl

The capital city, known for its serene environment, local markets, and picturesque hills.

Serchhip

Home to the highest waterfall in Mizoram, the Vantawng Falls, and breathtaking landscapes.

Lunglei

A tranquil town known for its scenic beauty and a perfect spot for nature lovers.

Champhai

Famous for its vineyards, rolling hills, and the ancient Mizo history.

Phawngpui

Known as the Blue Mountain of Mizoram, it is a paradise for trekkers and wildlife enthusiasts.

Reiek

A beautiful village surrounded by lush greenery and offering a panoramic view of the countryside.

Durtlang Hills

Offering a stunning view of Aizawl city and its surroundings, perfect for photography.

Tamdil Lake

A serene lake ideal for picnics and boating, surrounded by verdant forests.
