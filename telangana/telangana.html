Discover the Wonders of Telangana

Telangana, a state rich in history and culture, is home to stunning architecture, lush landscapes, and vibrant traditions. Explore its majestic forts, serene lakes, and natural wonders.

Top Destinations

Charminar

A historic monument in Hyderabad, symbolizing the rich heritage and architectural brilliance of Telangana.

Ramoji Film City

The largest film city in the world, offering stunning sets, gardens, and entertainment for visitors.

Warangal Fort

An ancient architectural wonder showcasing exquisite carvings and rich history.

Nagarjuna Sagar

One of the tallest masonry dams, surrounded by serene landscapes and lush greenery.

Bhadrakali Temple

An ancient temple dedicated to Goddess Bhadrakali, located by a serene lake.

Kuntala Waterfalls

The highest waterfall in Telangana, nestled amidst dense forests and rocky terrain.

Medak Cathedral

A magnificent church known for its Gothic architecture and serene ambiance.

Pakhal Lake

A man-made lake offering picturesque views and a tranquil retreat in nature.
