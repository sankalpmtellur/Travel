Discover the Wonders of Maharashtra

Maharashtra, a state of diverse culture and stunning landscapes, offers everything from vibrant cities and historical forts to serene beaches and hill stations.

Top Destinations

Mumbai

The bustling financial capital of India, famous for Bollywood, Gateway of India, and Marine Drive.

Bhandardara

A scenic hill station and holiday resort village, Bhandardara is known for its serene lakes, waterfalls, and lush greenery. It's a perfect destination for nature lovers and trekkers.

Ajanta Caves

An archaeological marvel showcasing ancient Buddhist art and architecture.

Bandra Kurla Complex (BKC)

BKC is a premier commercial and tech hub in Mumbai, featuring state-of-the-art corporate offices, technology centers, and a vibrant urban landscape. It also hosts cultural events and conferences, making it a must-visit for tech and business enthusiasts.

TCS Sahyadri Park

Located in Pune, TCS Sahyadri Park is Tata Consultancy Services' largest tech campus in India. Nestled amidst lush surroundings, it is a symbol of innovation and excellence, offering a blend of modern architecture and scenic views.

Lonavala

A popular weekend getaway, offering lush greenery and beautiful lakes.

Shirdi

The spiritual town associated with Sai Baba, attracting pilgrims from around the world.

Elephanta Caves

Located on Elephanta Island near Mumbai, these UNESCO World Heritage caves are known for their intricate rock-cut sculptures and ancient temples dedicated to Lord Shiva.
