Discover the Wonders of Andhra Pradesh

Andhra Pradesh, located in the southeastern part of India, is known for its rich culture, historic temples, and scenic coastline. From the famous Tirupati Balaji Temple to the serene beaches of Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh offers a blend of spirituality, adventure, and heritage.

Top Destinations

Tirupati

Home to the iconic Tirumala Venkateswara Temple.

Ananthagiri Hills

A serene hill station known for its lush greenery, coffee plantations, and tranquil trekking trails.

Gandikota

Known as the Grand Canyon of India, this stunning gorge is famous for its dramatic landscape and historic fort.

Lepakshi

Renowned for its stunning Veerabhadra Temple, intricate carvings, and the legendary hanging pillar, Lepakshi is a treasure trove of history and mythology.

Lambasingi

Often referred to as the 'Kashmir of Andhra Pradesh,' Lambasingi is known for its chilly weather, lush greenery, and scenic beauty. A perfect retreat for nature lovers.

Srikalahasti

Known as the 'Dakshina Kailasam,' Srikalahasti is a renowned temple town famous for its ancient Shiva temple, exquisite architecture, and spiritual significance. It is also a hub for Kalamkari art.

Araku Valley

A picturesque hill station known for its lush green valleys, coffee plantations, and tribal culture. Araku is perfect for nature lovers and offers stunning waterfalls, caves, and trekking trails.

Talakona Waterfalls

Talakona is the highest waterfall in Andhra Pradesh, located in the Sri Venkateswara National Park. Surrounded by lush forests, the waterfall is a popular spot for trekking and nature lovers. It's a serene getaway with an enchanting view.
