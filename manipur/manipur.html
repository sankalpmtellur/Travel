Discover the Wonders of Manipur

Manipur, nestled in the northeastern part of India, is known as the 'Jewel of India.' It boasts serene landscapes, vibrant culture, and rich history, offering travelers a unique blend of tradition and natural beauty.

Top Destinations

Loktak Lake

Known as the world's only floating lake, Loktak Lake is famous for its unique phumdis (floating islands).

Keibul Lamjao National Park

The only floating national park in the world, home to the endangered Sangai deer.

Ima Keithel

A unique women's market in Imphal, where all vendors are women, offering handicrafts, textiles, and local produce.

Kangla Fort

A historic site in Imphal, showcasing Manipur's cultural heritage and ancient artifacts.

Ukhrul

A picturesque hill station known for its serene environment and Shirui Lily, a rare flower.

Dzukou Valley

Known as the 'Valley of Flowers of the Northeast,' Dzukou Valley offers breathtaking landscapes and is a trekker's paradise.

Shilloi Lake

A serene and picturesque lake surrounded by lush hills, believed to be sacred by locals.

Andro Village

A cultural village near Imphal showcasing traditional pottery, crafts, and Manipuri heritage.
