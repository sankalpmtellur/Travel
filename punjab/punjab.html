Discover the Wonders of Punjab

Punjab, the land of five rivers, is known for its vibrant culture, rich history, and heartwarming hospitality. From golden temples to lush green fields, it offers an unforgettable experience.

Top Destinations

Golden Temple

The iconic spiritual site in Amritsar, known for its stunning architecture and serene atmosphere.

Wagah Border

Witness the vibrant and patriotic Beating Retreat ceremony at this historic border.

Jallianwala Bagh

A historic site commemorating the martyrs of the 1919 massacre, showcasing the spirit of freedom.

Anandpur Sahib

Known as the "Holy City of Bliss," Anandpur Sahib is a significant Sikh pilgrimage site, famous for its majestic gurdwaras and vibrant Hola Mohalla festival.

Ropar Wetlands

A paradise for bird watchers and nature enthusiasts, offering scenic beauty and tranquility.

Chandigarh

A planned city famous for its modern architecture, Rock Garden, and Sukhna Lake.

Harike Wetland and Bird Sanctuary

A paradise for bird watchers and nature enthusiasts, Harike Wetland is home to numerous migratory birds and a diverse ecosystem.

Phagwara

A city showcasing the perfect blend of Punjab's industrial progress and cultural heritage.
