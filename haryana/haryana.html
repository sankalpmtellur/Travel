Discover the Wonders of Haryana

Haryana, located in northern India, is known for its vibrant culture, historical significance, and picturesque landscapes. From the historic Kurukshetra to the modern city of Gurgaon, Haryana offers a unique blend of heritage and modernity.

Top Destinations

Kurukshetra

The land of the Mahabharata, Kurukshetra is a sacred city known for its historical and spiritual significance.

Pinjore Gardens

A Mughal-style garden with fountains, historic structures, and vibrant greenery.

Sultanpur Bird Sanctuary

A haven for bird watchers, home to numerous migratory and resident bird species.

Karnal

Known as the 'Rice Bowl of India,' Karnal boasts lush agricultural fields and historic sites.

Morni Hills

Haryana's only hill station, offering tranquil surroundings, trekking trails, and scenic beauty.

Yadavindra Gardens

A historic garden in Pinjore, showcasing terraced lawns, fountains, and vibrant flowers.

Gurgaon

A bustling city known for its skyscrapers, malls, and entertainment hubs.

Damdama Lake

A popular picnic spot offering boating, camping, and breathtaking views of the Aravalli hills.
