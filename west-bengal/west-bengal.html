Discover the Wonders of West Bengal

West Bengal is a state in eastern India, famous for its rich culture, history, and natural beauty. From the majestic Himalayas to the tranquil Sundarbans, West Bengal offers a variety of travel experiences for all types of explorers.

Top Destinations

Darjeeling

Known for its tea gardens, the iconic Darjeeling Himalayan Railway, and stunning views of the Kanchenjunga peak.

Sundarbans

The largest mangrove forest in the world, famous for its Royal Bengal Tigers and serene waterways.

Sylvan Sanctuary

A peaceful wildlife sanctuary located near the Himalayas, ideal for nature lovers and bird watchers.

Bellevue Waterfalls

One of the most beautiful waterfalls in West Bengal, located in the lush green hills.

Konark Temple

A magnificent ancient temple known for its intricate carvings and architecture, dedicated to the Sun God.

Kalimpong

A hill station known for its colonial architecture, monasteries, and the view of the Himalayan peaks.

Shantiniketan

Founded by Rabindranath Tagore, this cultural hub offers art, literature, and spiritual learning experiences.

Digha

A popular beach town on the Bay of Bengal known for its long sandy beaches and peaceful environment.
