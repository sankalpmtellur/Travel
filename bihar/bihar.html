Discover the Wonders of Bihar

Bihar, a land of ancient heritage and spiritual significance, offers a mix of historical landmarks, pilgrimage sites, and natural beauty. From the sacred Mahabodhi Temple in Bodh Gaya to the enchanting landscapes of Valmiki National Park, Bihar is a treasure trove for travelers.

Top Destinations

Mahabodhi Temple

A UNESCO World Heritage Site and a major Buddhist pilgrimage site in Bodh Gaya.

Nalanda University

An ancient seat of learning and one of the world's oldest universities.

Valmiki National Park

A pristine wildlife sanctuary and a tiger reserve located along the India-Nepal border.

Rajgir

A town with spiritual significance, known for its hot springs and Buddhist relics.

Vikramshila University

An ancient university that showcases the architectural brilliance of the Pala Empire.

Buddha Smriti Park

Located in Patna, Buddha Smriti Park is a serene urban park dedicated to the life and teachings of Buddha, featuring a stupa, meditation center, and museum.

Bihar Museum

A modern museum in Patna, the Bihar Museum showcases the rich cultural and historical heritage of Bihar with artifacts, sculptures, and interactive exhibits.

Sher Shah Suri Tomb

Situated in Sasaram, this magnificent tomb is a fine example of Indo-Islamic architecture, built in honor of the great emperor Sher Shah Suri.
