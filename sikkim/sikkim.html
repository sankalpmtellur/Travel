Discover the Wonders of Sikkim

Sikkim, nestled in the Eastern Himalayas, is a land of breathtaking natural beauty, vibrant culture, and serene monasteries. Known for its stunning landscapes and tranquility, Sikkim offers a magical escape into nature.

Top Destinations

Tsomgo Lake

A glacial lake surrounded by rugged mountains, reflecting the stunning Himalayan beauty.

Gurudongmar Lake

One of the highest lakes in the world, known for its pristine blue waters and spiritual significance.

Rumtek Monastery

A grand monastery showcasing Sikkim's rich Buddhist heritage and exquisite architecture.

Kanchenjunga Base Camp

An adventure haven offering close views of the majestic Kanchenjunga, the third-highest peak in the world.

Nathu La Pass

A historic mountain pass connecting India and Tibet, offering panoramic views and snow-clad trails.

Yuksom

A serene village steeped in history, offering trekking trails and a gateway to the Kanchenjunga National Park.

Zero Point

The last outpost of civilization, surrounded by snow-covered mountains and untouched beauty.

Namchi

Known for the towering statue of Guru Padmasambhava and spectacular views of the mountains.
