Discover the Wonders of Assam

Assam, nestled in the northeastern region of India, is known for its rich culture, tea gardens, and wildlife sanctuaries. From the famous Kaziranga National Park to the scenic beauty of Majuli, Assam is a land of natural wonders and cultural heritage.

Top Destinations

Kaziranga National Park

Famous for its one-horned rhinoceros and rich wildlife.

Majuli

The world's largest river island, known for its monasteries and vibrant culture.

Kakochang Waterfall

Located near Kaziranga, Kakochang Waterfall offers breathtaking views amidst lush greenery, making it a must-visit spot for nature lovers.

Manas National Park

A UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its diverse flora and fauna.

Sivasagar

Known for its historical monuments and as the center of Ahom culture.

Guwahati Planetarium

A hub for astronomy enthusiasts, the Guwahati Planetarium offers engaging shows and celestial experiences, perfect for visitors of all ages.

Haflong Lake

A serene and picturesque spot, Haflong Lake is a perfect destination for boating and enjoying the tranquil beauty of nature in Assam's only hill station.

Bihu Festival

The most famous cultural festival of Assam, celebrating the Assamese New Year.
