Discover the Wonders of Uttar Pradesh

Uttar Pradesh, known for its historical landmarks and spiritual destinations, offers a rich cultural experience. From the holy city of Varanasi to the majestic Taj Mahal, this state is a haven for history buffs and spiritual seekers alike.

Top Destinations

Taj Mahal

One of the Seven Wonders of the World, a symbol of eternal love and architectural beauty.

Varanasi Ghats

The spiritual heart of India, with sacred ghats on the Ganges and rich cultural heritage.

Agra Fort

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, offering stunning views of the Taj Mahal and Mughal history.

Sarnath

One of the holiest Buddhist sites, where Buddha gave his first sermon.

Khajuraho Temples

Famous for its ancient temples with intricate erotic sculptures and remarkable architecture.

Mathura and Vrindavan

Birthplaces of Lord Krishna, known for their temples, festivals, and spiritual significance.

Ram Mandir, Ayodhya

Ram Mandir is a sacred temple dedicated to Lord Ram, located in the holy city of Ayodhya. The temple holds great significance for Hindus and is a symbol of faith and devotion, situated at the birthplace of Lord Ram.

Lucknow

Known for its rich Mughal and Awadhi architecture, and the iconic Bara Imambara.
